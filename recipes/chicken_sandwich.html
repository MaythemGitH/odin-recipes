Chili-Honey Fried Chicken & Cornbread Waffle Sandwich

Home

Description

Crispy buttermilk fried chicken tucked between two sweet cornbread waffles, drizzled with sticky chili-honey glaze. It’s spicy, sweet, crunchy, and totally outrageous — brunch will never be the same.

Ingredients

For the Fried Chicken:
  • 2 boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
For the Cornbread Waffles:
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
For the Chili-Honey Glaze:
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1–2 tsp hot sauce or chili flakes (to taste)
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar

Instructions

1. Prep and Fry the Chicken:
  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk, paprika, and garlic powder for at least 1 hour (overnight best).
  2. Heat oil in a pan to 175°C (350°F). Dredge chicken in seasoned flour.
  3. Fry until golden and crispy (5–6 minutes per side). Drain on paper towel.
2. Make the Cornbread Waffles:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl. Whisk wet ingredients in another.
  2. Combine and mix until just smooth. Pour into preheated waffle iron.
  3. Cook until golden and firm. Keep warm.
3. Make the Chili-Honey Glaze:
  1. In a small saucepan, gently heat honey, chili, and vinegar. Stir and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
4. Assemble the Sandwich:
  1. Place a piece of fried chicken between two cornbread waffles.
  2. Drizzle with chili-honey glaze. Add pickles or slaw if desired. Devour immediately.
