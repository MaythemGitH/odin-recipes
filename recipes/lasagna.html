Lasagna

Description

A hearty Italian classic featuring layers of tender pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. Perfect for a comforting family dinner.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or beef/pork mix
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
For the Béchamel Sauce:
  • 50g (1/4 cup) butter
  • 50g (1/4 cup) all-purpose flour
  • 750ml (3 cups) milk
  • Salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg
For Assembly:
  • 9–12 lasagna sheets (fresh or no-boil)
  • 200g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 50g (1/2 cup) grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Make the Meat Sauce:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1–2 minutes to form a roux.
  2. Gradually add milk while whisking. Cook until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
Assemble and Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Spread a little meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish.
  2. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by meat sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, finishing with béchamel and both cheeses on top.
  3. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 15–20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  4. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
