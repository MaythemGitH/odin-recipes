Mango Coconut Sticky Rice Spring Rolls

Home

Description

A bold fusion twist on a Thai classic! Creamy coconut sticky rice and sweet ripe mango wrapped in delicate rice paper, served fresh with a drizzle of coconut sauce and sesame seeds. Tropical, chewy, crunchy, and unexpected.

Ingredients

For the Coconut Sticky Rice:
  • 1 cup glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
For Assembly:
  • 1–2 ripe mangoes, thinly sliced
  • 8–10 rice paper wrappers
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • Toasted sesame seeds (for garnish)
For Coconut Sauce (optional):
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (mixed with 1 tbsp water)

Instructions

Make the Coconut Sticky Rice:
  1. Rinse the rice several times until the water runs clear. Soak in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Steam the soaked rice for 25–30 minutes until tender.
  3. While rice cooks, heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a saucepan (do not boil). Stir until sugar dissolves.
  4. Combine cooked rice with the warm coconut mixture. Cover and let sit for 20 minutes.
Make the Coconut Sauce (optional):
  1. In a small saucepan, heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Stir in cornstarch slurry and simmer until thickened.
Assemble the Spring Rolls:
  1. Soften one rice paper sheet in warm water for 10–15 seconds. Lay it flat on a clean surface.
  2. Add a spoonful of sticky rice, a few mango slices, and mint if using.
  3. Fold in the sides and roll tightly like a burrito. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  4. Serve with coconut sauce and a sprinkle of sesame seeds. Best enjoyed fresh!
